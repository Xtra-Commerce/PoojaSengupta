Pooja Sengupta is a marketplace and ecommerce executive with 17+ years of experience building and scaling marketplaces globally. She has managed P&L exceeding $1.1 billion and has expertise in marketplace strategy, ecommerce leadership, and founder advisory. Her experience spans the United States, Australia, and India, with work at leading organizations including Coles (Wesfarmers, Fortune 500), Catch Marketplace, GS1 Australia, and Lasoo.com.au (IVE Group). Currently, she is the Founder of PurvX. Key achievements include transforming Lasoo from a digital catalogue to a full-scale marketplace, leading the Coles √ó eBay partnership launching the world's first grocery marketplace on eBay, and driving merchandising and platform growth at Catch.com.au. She holds an MBA from Swinburne University and a Bachelor of Engineering from Anna University.
