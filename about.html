About Pooja Sengupta

Marketplace Executive, Founder & Ecommerce Leader

Professional Journey

Pooja is a marketplace and ecommerce executive with 17+ years of experience building and scaling global digital commerce platforms. Her career spans web solutions, digital marketing analytics, enterprise ecommerce consulting, and billion-dollar marketplace operations across North America, Australia, and India.

In the early stages of her career, Pooja worked in web solutions and marketing analytics, establishing foundational expertise in digital strategy. She then transitioned into ecommerce consulting, advising businesses across India, the United States, and the United Kingdom on platform optimization and growth strategy.

Her enterprise leadership experience includes transformative roles at two of Australia's largest ecommerce platforms. At Catch.com.au, she headed merchandising operations for a $200M portfolio, driving margin expansion and platform growth on the Mirakl marketplace infrastructure. She then joined Coles Supermarket as Head of Online Merchandising, overseeing a $1.1B P&L with 800+ storefronts and leading strategic partnerships across UberEATS, Airtasker, and Deliveroo.

At GS1 Australia, Pooja served as Account Director for eCommerce, developing and managing strategic partnerships with Amazon, Google, and Alibaba on behalf of 300+ brands. This role sharpened her expertise in data standards, product information management, and cross-border ecommerce enablement.

Her recent marketplace transformation work at Lasoo.com.au demonstrated her ability to execute complex platform migrations, scaling the business from catalog to marketplace model with 60+ retail partners and achieving 120% month-over-month GMV growth.

Today, Pooja is Founder and CEO of PurvX, a circular fashion marketplace focused on South Asian designers and conscious consumers. Operating from San Francisco with engineering and operations in Chennai, PurvX has achieved 800% user growth and 3.2x seller conversion within its first year, pioneering sustainable models in fashion ecommerce.

She brings deep expertise in marketplace strategy, seller enablement, buyer acquisition, data standards, and building culturally informed commerce experiences at scale.
